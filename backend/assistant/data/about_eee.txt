The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE) offers U.G and P.G programs in Electrical Engineering Discipline. The Department is recognized as a Research Centre in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The research work in the department is vibrant with the support of eminent professors like Dr.M.Rama Moorthy, former Director General, CPRI taking a keen interest in guiding the faculty members. The research interests of the faculty members encompass a wide gamut of sub-disciplines of Electrical Engineering. The Department takes pride in its ability to nurture students who win gold medals at the university level consistently. We also take up the social responsibility of inculcating awareness about Energy Conservation by promoting programs about the same. Collaboration with industries for timely amendments of curriculum and laboratories is another credential of the department.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE), established in 1995,
offers a B.Tech. program in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and two M.Tech.
programs specializing in Power Electronics and Power Systems. The B.Tech. program
is accredited for six years, while the M.Tech. (Power Electronics) program holds a threeyear accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). Recognized as a
Research Center by JNTU Hyderabad, the department comprises 42 highly qualified
faculty members, including 24 Ph.D. holders and 15 pursuing doctoral research,
guiding nine Ph.D. scholars. Equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, the
department maintains strong industry and academic collaborations through MoUs
with reputed institutions and organizations.
The Department offers minor degree in:
• Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
• Internet of Things
• Cyber Security
• Data Science
• Innovation and Entrepreneurship

INTERNSHIPS AND PLACEMENTS
Students undergo a mandatory 45 days internship program after 6th semester. Students
go as intern to companies like Schnieder, DRDO, BHEL, AP GENCO, TRANSCO and to premiere
institutions like IIT’s.
Students receive annual training in core areas such as Renewable Energy, Electric Vehicles,
Embedded Systems, and Automation, as well as in software domains including Soft Skills, Aptitude,
Basic and Advanced Coding, and Machine Learning. Our students secure placements in PSUs, the
IT industry, and various state and central government organizations with competitive salary
packages
Additionally, our students have also got internship in firms like Worley Services India pvt ltd,
NPCIL Kaiga Site, Axis Energy pvt ltd and CPRI. We have also provided on campus internship
opportunities on autonomous vehicles in collaboration with Magarita Tech
